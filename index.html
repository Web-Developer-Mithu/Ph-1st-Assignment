Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DevConf 2026

When and where is DevConf 2026 taking place?

+

DevConf 2026 will take place from September 10-12, 2026 at the San Francisco Convention Center, 1301 S Mission St, San Francisco, CA 94103. Early-bird pricing ends August 15, 2026.

What's included in each ticket tier?

+

Standard ($399): Access to 3 days, 48 talks, 2 workshops, lunch, coffee breaks, swag, and 30-day recordings.

Pro ($749): Everything in Standard plus unlimited workshops, speaker meet & greet, VIP dinner, lifetime recordings, and 30-min mentorship.

Team ($5,999): 10 Pro passes with dedicated lounge, private workshops, company logo, recruitment booth, and priority support.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

+

Refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Tickets purchased within 30 days of the conference are non-refundable but transferable to another attendee.

Will there be virtual attendance options?

+

Yes! Virtual passes are available for $199. Virtual attendees get access to all recorded talks for 1 year, live Q&A sessions, and digital networking lounges. Workshops are in-person only.

How do I prepare for the workshops?

+

Workshop details and prerequisites will be sent to registered attendees 2 weeks before the event. We recommend bringing a laptop and installing the required tools in advance. All materials will be provided in our pre-event kit.

Is there a student or early-career discount?

+

Yes! Students get 40% off any ticket tier with valid student ID. Early-career professionals (0-2 years) get 25% off. To claim your discount, contact support@devconf2026.com with proof of status.
